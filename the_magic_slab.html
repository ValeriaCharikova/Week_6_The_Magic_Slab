The Slab is based on a simple, nostalgic idea: paying tribute to the original
American ice cream parlor – built on quality ice cream, delicious toppings
and a fun and inviting experience.

Supporting our local farmers

Ice cream with the look and taste of an old favorite. Every bite melts in your
mouth to leave a creamy, rich aftertaste. All of this is a direct result of our
support for local farms with the best ingredients. Supplied fresh every
morning, for every batch.
